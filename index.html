About ABM GROUPS

ABM GROUPS, proudly rooted in Kottakupam, is a dynamic business collective dedicated to serving the diverse needs of communities across Pondicherry and Tamil Nadu. Our mission is to foster growth, innovation, and community well-being through our varied enterprises. We strive to deliver exceptional value and build lasting relationships, driven by a commitment to quality and a deep understanding of our local markets.

From education and technology to real estate and social welfare, ABM GROUP is committed to making a positive impact, guided by our core values of integrity, excellence, and community focus.
